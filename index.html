I'm Sri, a product management leader currently at Zalando, Europe's leading fashion e-commerce platform.

I lead livestream commerce and product management initiatives as Head of Product, managing cross-functional teams across European and China engineering organizations. My work sits at the intersection of product leadership, international team management, and technical platform development: building scalable commerce experiences that connect content, technology, and customer engagement.

Over 15+ years in consumer and B2B tech product management, I've focused on platform building, international commerce, and increasingly, AI and content products in retail and consumer tech.

Previously, I spent nearly 6 years at Amazon Luxembourg, working across multiple product domains: launching Amazon Business to Italy and Spain, managing identity verification and registration products, and later driving Business Prime partnerships and integrations across US and EU markets. I hold an MBA from INSEAD, one of the world's leading business schools, with emphasis on cross-cultural collaboration and strategic thinking.

Current Focus

At Zalando, I'm executing on a new Inspiration & Entertainment strategy in e-commerce, through livestreaming across European markets. Beyond the day-to-day execution, I'm interested in AI automation for product workflows. I build tools to ease my life: learning AI through practice while optimizing my work and personal life. My 2026 resolution is to write my learnings every weekend, documenting what I'm building and learning.

An Unusual Path

My background is unconventional for product management. I started with a Chemistry honors degree at National University of Singapore,in analytical chemistry and synthetic nanochemistry in undergrad. The pivot to technology management came through the Stanford-NUS Overseas Colleges program, I subsequently joined a startup (tenCube) and taught myself programming and technical fundamentals along the way.

This chemistry-to-tech trajectory gave me comfort with scientific thinking, technical depth, and the ability to learn new domains quickly. The INSEAD MBA later added commercial acumen, financial fluency, and strategic frameworks: evolving me from pure technical execution to leadership and business strategy.
